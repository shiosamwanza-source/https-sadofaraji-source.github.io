About the Author

Sado Faraji is a distinguished author and historian whose works span historical biography, religious critique, and literary fiction. With a focus on African history, Islamic civilization, and social justice, Faraji's writings challenge conventional narratives and offer fresh perspectives on power, resistance, and human dignity.
